Our Journey: Planting Seeds of Knowledge

On March 13, 2019, Saachi Foundation embarked on a profound mission: to establish a school where underprivileged and economically disadvantaged children could access quality education without discrimination. This vision gave birth to Kids Gardens Play School. We started in a rented shed, lacking proper facilities, but fueled by an unwavering dream and dedication.

Humble Beginnings

Started in a rented cement shed, facing extreme weather conditions with leaking roofs and sweltering heat, yet students persevered.

Unwavering Dedication

Despite the challenges, our commitment to providing education remained strong, driven by the hope for a brighter future for every child.

A Dream Realized

From a small shed, Kids Gardens Play School emerged as a testament to the power of education and community support.

Overcoming Challenges: A New Chapter

Our school faces a critical juncture. The five-year lease on our current land has expired, and with the unfortunate demise of the landowner, we are compelled to vacate the premises immediately. This necessitates a move to a permanent location.

Imagine a space where no child is denied education due to poverty. A place where students from PG to 8th grade thrive in an environment rich with AI, skill development, technology, and modern learning. We strive to provide not just books, but a pathway to a bright future.

Our Vision: Building a Brighter Future

Our goal is ambitious yet vital: to acquire new land and construct a healthy, safe, and technologically equipped school building. This permanent home will ensure uninterrupted, quality education for all.
