Our Journey: Planting Seeds of Knowledge

On March 15, 2019, Saadhvi Foundation embarked on a profound mission to establish a school where underprivileged and economically disadvantaged children could access quality education without discrimination. This vision gave birth to Kids Gardens Play School. We started in a rented shed, lacking proper facilities, but fueled by an unwavering dream and dedication.

Humble Beginnings

Started in a rented cement shed, facing extreme weather conditions with leaking roofs and sweltering heat, yet students persevered.

Unwavering Dedication

Despite the challenges, our commitment to providing education remained strong, driven by the belief in a brighter future for every child.

A Dream Realized

From a small shed, Kids Gardens Play School emerged as a testament to the power of education and community support.

Overcoming Challenges: A New Chapter

Our school faces a critical juncture. The five-year lease on our current land has expired, and with the unfortunate demise of the landowner, we find ourselves in an uncertain position. This necessitates a move to a permanent location.

Despite a space where no child is denied education due to poverty, it lacks essential infrastructure. We strive to provide an environment with AI, skill development, technology, and an innovative approach. We strive to provide not just books, but a pathway to a bright future.

Our Vision: Building a Brighter Future

Our goal is ambitious yet vital: to acquire new land and construct a healthy, safe, and technologically equipped school building. This permanent home will ensure uninterrupted, quality education for all.

Acquire New Land

Secure a permanent site for our school's future.

Construct Modern Building

Create a safe, healthy, and tech-ready learning environment.

Advanced Curriculum

Offer AI, coding, IT skills, spoken English, and employability training.

Inclusive Education

Provide quality education from PG to 12th grade, without discrimination.

We stand at a critical juncture where our current challenges threaten to jeopardize the future of our children. The rented building is no longer secure, and we lack the own land. However, we still possess your trust, your support, and our unwavering determination.
